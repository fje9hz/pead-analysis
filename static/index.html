TL;DR

Companies report earnings every quarter. Analysts make predictions beforehand. When a company beats those predictions by a lot, its stock doesn't just jump on the day — it keeps rising for weeks. This project measures that pattern using 13 years of real Wall Street data, then builds a model to predict which companies are most likely to beat or miss before they announce.
